Get in touch — the SOT community is ready to help

Contact the SOT

The Ship Observations Team is a community of national meteorological services, programme managers, Port Meteorological Officers, scientists, and technical experts spread across more than 50 countries. Whether you are a shipping company exploring how to join the VOS Scheme, an officer with an instrument question at sea, a researcher needing data access information, or a national service looking to expand its programme — there is a contact in the SOT community who can help.

For general enquiries about the Ship Observations Team, its programmes, or how to participate, please contact the SOT Technical Coordinator based at OceanOPS (Brest, France).
